Description

CTF, or Capture The Flag, is a cybersecurity competition where participants engage in challenges to discover, exploit, and defend vulnerabilities in computer systems. It simulates real-world scenarios, requiring participants to solve puzzles, break codes, and exploit weaknesses to "capture flags" or specific pieces of information. CTFs serve as a platform for honing cybersecurity skills and fostering a competitive yet collaborative environment in the field.

Rules
