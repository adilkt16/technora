Battlegrounds Mobile India is a popular mobile battle royale game,offering an immersive gaming experience where players compete with each other until only one remains.it shares intense game play realistic,graphics and a diverse range of weapons and strategies

Rules

⚪️ It is a 4(+1) player team tournament. A 4-man starter roster and up to 1 substitute.

⚪️ Event will be conducted on Feb 18th and 19th after 7 pm

⚪️ Point System : 10 Points for WWCD & 1 Point per kill

⚪️ Emulators are not allowed in any game mode organized. The player will be disqualified if found using any kind of emulator.

⚪️ Any game modifying tools except "GFX tool" is not allowed.

⚪️ Players can play on iOS/Android mobile devices only. iPads/Android tablets are strictly disallowed.

⚪️ Only in-game voice chat should be used after the game is started till its completion.

⚪️ Any use of unfair means such as aimbot, trigger bot, ESP will result in disqualification.

⚪️ Should a team/player fail to join the room in time, their squad/they will be given 0 points for it.

⚪️ Waiting time is at most 5 minutes between games.

⚪️ Exiting a game without a good reason will disqualify the team.

⚪️ The exploitation of bugs that hinders fair play will result in disqualification.

⚪️ For the tiebreaker of the points, the number of chicken dinners will be considered.

⚪️ For the further tiebreaker, the total number of team kills will be considered.

⚪️ Organizers would not be held responsible for connectivity issues on the participant's side.

⚪️ The entry fee would not be refunded under any circumstances.

⚪️ Organizers reserve the right to accept or reject any entry without stating a reason thereof.

⚪️ Participants are requested to remain ready at least 15 minutes prior to the start of any match. Late entries would not be allowed.

⚪️ At least two players in the team currently playing are required to record their gameplay with in-game audio. In case of any complaints from the opponents, these players are required to send their POV recording. Failure to do so will result in disqualification.
