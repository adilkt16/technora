Description

Spot videography involves capturing spontaneous and candid moments with a handheld camera, emphasising real-time, unscripted footage. This style aims to authentically document events or scenes . Spot videography is valued for its ability to capture the essence of the moment, offering viewers a more immediate and unfiltered experience.
