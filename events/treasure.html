Description

Treasure Hunt is a recreational activity where participants follow a set of clues or a map to locate hidden treasures or objects.This adventure promotes participants to decipher clues and navigate through the designated area in pursuit of the hidden reward.

Date: 20 / 2 /2024

Time: 01:00pm - 03:30pm

Rules

⚪️ Each team must have a minimum of 2 members and maximum 4 members.

⚪️ All team members must stay together and don't split up.

⚪️ The hunt time will be 2 hours. From 1:30pm - 3:30pm.

⚪️ All teams must reach the open stage before 1:30

⚪️ The game will start at exact 1:30pm and will end at exact 3:30pm.

⚪️ Those who didn't reach the starting point by 1:30 pm will have less time to complete the hunt.

⚪️ All teams will be provided with a QR code at the starting point which will lead you to your first location.

⚪️ There will be a total of 10 levels and an another final level in this game.

⚪️ Each location (except the first location ) and is locked with a unique code.these codes will be provided in the last level and all those who have entered the code can only access the next location clues.

⚪️ In each level there will also be a puzzle piece . All teams should collect these pieces as after the final level to reach the finish,you should solve this puzzle as this puzzle will give you the clues to the final location.

⚪️ Those team who reaches the final location first will be rewarded first and the next one reaching after that will be rewarded second.

⚪️ There will only be 2 winners in this event. First and the second.

⚪️ Don't search for clues in off-limits areas . These places include library, prayer hall, staffrooms, offices , avt room, seminar hall and all kinds of labs.

⚪️ The clues are to be found in a particular order. A team cannot skip a clue. Skipping clues leads to the disqualification of that team.

⚪️ If participants move any objectto search for clues . Kindly please place it back.

⚪️ Don't tamper or destroy QR codes . Destroying or tampering with these will lead to the disqualification of the team.

⚪️ For any doubt's you have , please ask with the treasure hunt volunteer who will be there .

⚪️ All participants are to keep in mind as to not disturb the other ongoing classes

⚪️ Make sure to complete the game before 3:30pm .
